Tracking Trump's Conflicts of Interest

Details and news coverage of the Trump Family’s business and financial dealings

President Donald Trump took office with an unprecedented number of potential conflicts of interest — business and financial ties that may conflict with his public duties as president of the United States. This project attempts to track all of the First Family’s potential conflicts of interest, as well as news coverage of them.

This database is part of the Sunlight Foundation’s “Tracking Trump’s Conflicts of Interest” project, funded by the Lodestar Foundation. You can learn more about the project here, and if you’re familiar with any of the conflicts we’re tracking, contact us here or contact us here to contribute to the project.
